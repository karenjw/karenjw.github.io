 Steamed Slippery Chicken with Mushrooms & Wood Ear 冬菇雲耳蒸滑雞

Cantonese style Steamed Chicken with Mushrooms & Wood Ear (冬菇雲耳蒸滑雞) is my boyfriend’s favorite dishes. He can not refuse steamed chicken, and he could finish three bowls of rice with this dish. Most importantly, steaming is one of the healthest cooking methods.

Ingredient

  • 1 lb. bonless chicken with skin (or with bone is your choice)
  • Dried wood ear mushrooms, fresh shiitake mushrooms
  • 1.5 inch(3.5-4cm) ginger
  • 2 Tbsp. light soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp. sesame oil
  • Salt, suger, garlic power, pepper(up to your taste)

Directions

  1. Rinse wood ear mushrooms and shiitake mushrooms separately under running water.
  2. And transfer them into bowls filled with water, and soak them for 1 hour.
  3. Transfer out and drain, cut them into pices.
  4. In a large bowl, mix the chicken and all mushrooms together with all the marinating ingredients.
  5. And then set aside to marinate for 30 minutes. (or over night, or 10 minutes, the marinate time is your choice)
  6. Place in a covered steamer, and steam over medium high heat for 20 minutes.
  7. Carefully remove from the steamer, then serve immediately with hot rice.

 Stir-Fried Squid with Broccoli 西蘭花炒鮮魷

Cantonese style Stir-Fried Squid with Broccoli (西蘭花炒鮮魷) is one of my favorite dish as well. It is a healthy dish with high nutritional value and low calories.

Ingredient

  • 2 cloves of garlic, 1 inch (2.5cm) ginger
  • 1 Tbsp. coconut oil, 1 Tbsp. sesame oil
  • Salt, garlic power, pepper(up to your taste)
  • 1 head Broccoli
  • 5-8 baby squid

Directions

  1. Clean the baby squid and cut it into pices, and marinade it in salt and pepper for 10 minutes.
  2. Cut the broccoli into small florets, and blanch it in boiling water for 5 minutes, then drain.
  3. Heat a large frying pan with coconut oil over high heat, add garlic and ginger.
  4. Stir-fry the squid until it turns opaque, add sesame oil and garlic power.
  5. Add the boiled broccoli into the frying pan, mixture with squid thoroughly for 1 minutes.
  6. Then serve immediately with hot rice.

 Ma-Po Tofu 麻婆豆腐

Ma-Po Tofu (麻婆豆腐) is one of my favorite dish. Since it takes less than 30 minutes to prepare, I make it into my dinner dish frequently for myself. It is a great healthy dish to please everyone in the family.

Ingredient

  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 inch (2.5cm) ginger
  • 0.5 Tbsp. coconut oil
  • 1 bag of Ma-Po Tofu sauce
  • 6 Tbsp water
  • 0.5 lb thin-cut pork belly ( or any other ground meet of your choice)
  • 14 oz softy tofu

Directions

  1. Cut the garlic and ginger into small pices. Drain the tofu and cut into small cubes.
  2. In a large fying pan, heat coconut oil on medium heat and saute garlic and ginger.
  3. Add the pork belley and break it up with chopsticks or wooden spoon.
  4. When the meat is no longer pink, add the tofu and stir thoroughly.
  5. Add the sauce and water, make meat and tofu coat the sauce, stir frequently.
  6. Once the sauce is back to boiling, continue to be heated for 3 minutes.
  7. Then serve immediately with hot rice.
